Dirigo runners opened Thanksgiving week with strong age-group results at the Maine Track Club Turkey Trot 5K in Cape Elizabeth.

Garth Altenburg won the men's 50-54 division, and Craig Robinson won the men's 30-34 division.

Good luck to everyone racing over Thanksgiving weekend, including athletes lining up at the Portland 4-Miler and Greely 5K.
