Marah Borgman won the Millinocket Half in 1:27:47. Sam Chick finished fifth in 1:20:54. In the full marathon, Jon McGonagle placed ninth in 3:08:42.

At the Ugly Sweater 5K, Cait Bourgault won the women's race in 18:00 with Veronica Graziano second in 18:15 and Michelle Lilienthal third in 18:31. Romayn Richards won the men's race in 16:01; Nick Denari was second in 16:41, Thomas Harvey third in 16:55, and Chris Harmon placed in 17:09.
