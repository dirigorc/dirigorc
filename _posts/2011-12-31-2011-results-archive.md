summary: "The recovered 2011 archive is anchored by two marathon Olympic Trials qualifiers: Erica Jesseman won Hartford in 2:45:00 and Sheri Piers won F40-44 at Twin Cities in 2:37:42. Dirigo's 40-49 masters and 50-59 veterans both won USATF New England Cross Country titles, and the masters team placed sixth at USATF National Club Cross Country. Beach to Beacon had Sheri Piers and Erica Jesseman as the top two Maine women, Kristin Barry fifth Maine woman, Josh Zolla fourth Maine man, and Mike Payson, Mark Goettel, and Pete Bottomley sweeping the Maine masters podium. The archive also includes Rob Ashby winning Bar Harbor and Komen Race for the Cure, Chris Harmon winning Dan Cardillo and Great Pumpkin, Mary Pardi winning Great Pumpkin and the Hartford half master title, Josh Zolla winning L.L.Bean and St. Peter's, and Erica Jesseman, Sheri Piers, Kristin Barry, Rob Gomez, Mike Payson, Jorma Kurry, and Pete Bottomley repeatedly appearing at the front of summer and fall results."
