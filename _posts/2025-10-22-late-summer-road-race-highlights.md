Dirigo carried summer momentum deep into August and September with wins, podiums, and strong series results.

David Ouellette took third at the Whiskey Road Half in Arizona, and Thomas Harvey won the Lincoln Half Marathon.

Justin Freeman put together one of the club's best late-summer streaks, finishing fourth at Race to the Top in Vermont, second master at the Falmouth 7 Miler, fifth at the Delta Dental Manchester 5K, fifth master at the USATF-NE 10K Championships, and first at the Delta Dental Mountain Challenge.

The Weekly Back Cove Series season also ended with two Dirigo titles, with Clíodhna O'Malley and Nick Denari both winning their overall summerlong competitions.
