summary: "The recovered 2020 archive is necessarily short, but it still preserves two clear Dirigo markers from a disrupted year. At the Maine Marathon COVID-19 Edition, Ryan Collins, Joe Satterfield, and Rob Ashby ran 2:37:22, 2:37:25, and 2:39:45. At the Mid-Winter 10-Mile Classic, Gretchen Speed won the women's race while Sam Seekins, Michael Gordon, Ryan Jara, Nick Denari, James Boeding, Chris Harmon, Mary Pardi, Sarah Baker, Kalie Dunn, Peter Burke, and many more Dirigo runners filled the results."
