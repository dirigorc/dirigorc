Marah Borgman won the Millinocket Half in 1:27:47. Sam Chick placed 5th in the half in 1:20:54. In the full marathon, Jon McGonagle finished 9th in 3:08:42.

At the Ugly Sweater 5K, Cait Bourgault won the women's race in 18:00, with Veronica Graziano second in 18:15 and Michelle Lilienthal third in 18:31. On the men's side, Romayn Richards won in 16:01, followed by Nick Denari (16:41), Thomas Harvey (16:55), and Chris Harmon (17:09).
