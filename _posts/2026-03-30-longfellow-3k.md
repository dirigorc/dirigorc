Dirigo had a strong afternoon at the Longfellow 3K on the peninsula in Portland, led by a front-running women's group.

Marah Borgman took the overall women's win, with Gretchen Speed second and Lauren Austin fifth to put three Dirigo women in the top five. Nedicus Swain also cracked the overall top 10 with seventh place.

The race also served as an early spring checkpoint with the Falmouth 4-Miler coming up next on the local calendar.
