Good crew tonight in the sun at Weekly Back Cove.

Quick hits: Nick Denari with another win and a sub-16 summer PB, and Dave Ouellette with a season PB smashing 19. Marah Borgman took the women's win, and Crow Norlander ran into the top 10.

Chris Harmon is getting back into it with a second straight week under 18. College-bound Grace Alexander made her 2026 summer debut, and Adane Bewset, Dan Bannon, Dave Ouellette, Seth Crockett, and Deven Abrams went 9 through 13.
