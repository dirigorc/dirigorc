Rich Fargo and Jeff Walker were standouts for Dirigo at the USATF National Classic Mountain Running Championship on June 10, 2025, at Mount Sunapee, NH.

- Rich Fargo (Chatham, NH) won the M65–69 division in 1:56:53 (age 66; overall place reported in results list: 142).
- Jeff Walker (Falmouth, ME) placed third in the M55–59 division in 1:39:38 (age 59; overall place reported in results list: 90).

The course was a two-loop, roughly 9+ mile route with steep ski-trail climbs, a wet and technical bouldery hiking-trail section near the second summit, and fast, muddy descents. Team members described the downhills as fun and fast despite the conditions.
