Dirigo's July 4 weekend started a day early with a front-of-the-pack result at the GRBA 5K, where Rob Gomez and Craig Robinson finished first and second on July 3.

On July 4, the team had strong results at races around the state. Heather Gallant led the women's field at Friends on the 4th in Winthrop, and Nate Priest finished inside the men's top 10.

At Bridgton 4 on the Fourth, Karley Piers was second woman, Taylor Bickford raced to fourth overall while wearing bib #1776, and Daniel Bannon was eighth overall and top masters.
