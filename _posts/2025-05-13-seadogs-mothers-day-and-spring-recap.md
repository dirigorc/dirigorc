Michelle Lilienthal won the SeaDogs Mother's Day 5K in 17:54. Teammates included Gretchen Speed (18:53, 4th), Kristin Barry (19:57, 7th and 1st W50-54), Laurie Nicholas (23:48, 1st F55-59), Nick Denari (15:59, 6th), Craig Robinson (16:08, 7th), Seth Crockett (17:26, 10th and 1st M35-39), Thomas Harvey (17:28, 11th and 2nd M35-39), Jon McGonagle (17:45, 13th and 3rd M40-44), and Nate Priest (21:44, with a stroller).

At Sugarloaf Marathon Mary Pardi ran 3:11:03 to place ninth and win F55-59. Sarah Baker ran 3:28:24, Rich Alff ran 2:44:00 (12th, 1st M50-54), Rob Ashby ran 2:44:07 (13th, 1st M55-59), and Zackary Jellis posted a PR in 3:52:28.

Veronica Graziano won the Sugarloaf 15K in 54:32. Keri Mayo was 30th in 1:13:46 (4th F45-49).

Local 5K winners and notable results: Jonathan Clement won the Run for Independence 5K in Brunswick in 18:48. At the Mental Health Matters 5K in Cumberland, Nathan Madeira won in 16:54, Sam Chick was third in 17:08, and Christie Hein won her race in 20:26.

Spencer McElwain won the Maine Coast Half Marathon in 1:09:34.
