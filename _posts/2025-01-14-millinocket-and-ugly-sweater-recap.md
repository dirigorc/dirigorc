Marah Borgman won the Millinocket Half Marathon in 1:27:47. Sam Chick was fifth in the half in 1:20:54, and Jon McGonagle finished ninth in the full in 3:08:42.

At the Ugly Sweater 5K, Cait Bourgault won the women's race in 18:00 with Veronica Graziano second (18:15) and Michelle Lilienthal third (18:31). Romayn Richards won the men's race in 16:01; Nick Denari was second in 16:41, Thomas Harvey third in 16:55, and Chris Harmon ran 17:09.

Full results were shared with the team via All Sports Events and the Ugly Sweater results page.
