summary: "Dirigo's masters group was at a high point: the team was third at USA National Club Cross Country and won the USATF New England masters title, with the open team also third. Beach to Beacon lists Patrick Tarpy as first Maine finisher, Rob Gomez third Maine finisher, Mike Bunker fifth, Kristin Barry and Sheri Piers as the top two Maine women, Carry Buterbaugh fifth Maine woman, Christine Reaser as female master winner, and age-group results from Josh Zolla, Blaine Moore, Andy Spaulding, Ethan Hemphill, Byrne Decker, Keelyn Wu, Rob Ashby, Mike Payson, Bill Mariski, Pete Bottomley, Marc Dugas, and Tom Ryan. Sheri Piers set a Mid-Winter Classic course record in 1:00:45, Rob Gomez, Josh Zolla, Kristin Barry, Sheri Piers, and Christine Reaser won the Run for Cash team title, Michael Bunker set a Breakaway 5K course record in 15:01, alongside wins from Josh Zolla, Todd Coffin, Rob Ashby, Byrne Decker, Patrick Fournier, Matt Lunt, Michael Bunker, Patrick Tarpy, Blaine Moore, Andrea Newton, and Kristin Barry."
