Dirigo runners closed out fall marathon season with strong results across major-city courses and regional championship fields.

At Berlin, Taylor Bickford and Travis Kroot both finished on a warm day in one of the season's toughest international marathon conditions.

Baystate brought another front-end result from Romayn Richards, while Kristin Barry added a strong marathon performance.

At the Detroit Free Press Marathon, Jon McGonagle ran 2:56:55 and placed fourth in his age group.

Chicago rounded out the month with a deep Dirigo group: Spencer McElwain ran 2:25:29 for 100th overall, Jesse Orach ran 2:41:32, Erin Chalat ran 3:22:28, and Gina Pardi ran 3:08:19.
