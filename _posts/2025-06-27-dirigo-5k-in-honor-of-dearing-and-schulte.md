The Dirigo 5K, run in honor of Dan Dearing and Shiloh Schulte, was held last Friday and continues to grow as a community distance event. Luke Marsanskis won the men's race in 14:31, and Bethanie Brown won the women's race in 17:07.

Several racers posted personal records: Jason Maher, James DeMuro, Seridel Hel, and Zack Jellis all ran PRs. Maine Running Photos shot a large gallery from the race (link above).

Thanks to everyone who ran and to the volunteers who made the day possible.
