Dirigo recorded team and individual highlights at Boston, Beach to Beacon, Loon Mountain, L.L.Bean 10K, Bridgton Four on the Fourth, Maine Marathon, USATF New England Masters Cross Country, and dozens of local road and trail races. The year includes wins and age-group results from Rob Ashby, Erica Jesseman, Gretchen Speed, Michael Gordon, Ryan Jara, Mary Pardi, Joe Reynolds, Chris Dunn, Michelle Lilienthal, Judson Cake, Sheri Piers, and many more runners who kept the blue singlet visible all season."
