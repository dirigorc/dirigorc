summary: "Dirigo closed November with strong results across Thanksgiving races and late-fall events. At the Portland Thanksgiving Day 4-Miler, Veronica Graziano won in 22:51, with Michelle Lilienthal second, Erica Jesseman fourth, Marah Borgman seventh, and Gretchen Speed eighth, while Rob Ashby, Crow Norlander, Jon McGonagle, and Tim Stickney led the men's side. Sheri Piers was second overall female and first 50+ at the Greely Turkey Trot, and Jonathan Clement returned in Dirigo colors with second place at the Maine Track Club Turkey Trot. Beyond Thanksgiving, Gretchen Speed won the Great Pumpkin 10K in 38:19, Dan Bannon was third in 34:15, Justin Freeman took second at the USATF men's masters 8K in Franklin Park, Sarah Baker and Keri Mayo PR'd at Philadelphia, Nathan Madeira ran 2:46:11 at NYC, and Cait Bourgault posted a 3:07:40 at Every Woman's Marathon before winning the Great Osprey 10K in 39:26."
