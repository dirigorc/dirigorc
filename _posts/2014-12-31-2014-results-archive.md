summary: "The recovered 2014 archive shows Dirigo racing from January through the holidays. At Boston, the women's masters team won overall, the men's masters team was fifth, Byrne Decker was first Maine male, Erica Jesseman was first Maine female, and Sheri Piers and Kristin Barry placed fourth and sixth among female masters. Beach to Beacon was another blue-heavy day: Erica Jesseman, Sheri Piers, and Kristin Barry were 2-3-5 among Maine women, Rob Gomez was fourth Maine man, Andy Spaulding and Sheri Piers were Maine masters winners, Bill Mariski was first Maine senior, and Mary Pardi won F40-44. The year also includes Erica Jesseman winning Hartford in 2:40:58, Chris Harmon and Jesse Hugo going 2-4 in the Maine Marathon, Sam Seekins winning the Maine Half, Rob Gomez and Erica Jesseman winning Trail to Ale, Spencer McElwain and Jenna Krajewski setting Old Hallowell course records, and Chris Harmon winning L.L.Bean, Bagel Boogie, Patriot's Day, Race Judicata, Jimmy the Greek's, and Scarborough Frozen."
