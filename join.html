Join Dirigo

Join the blue crew.

Dirigo membership is open to runners with a Maine connection who want to train, race, and contribute to the team.

Who should consider Dirigo?

Dirigo is for runners with a Maine connection who are committed to training, competition, teamwork, and good sportsmanship. There are no time or age requirements for joining.

The first step is simple: join or renew online, then the team can help you get connected.

What members should know.

Dues

Annual adult membership dues are $40. Dues help cover team registration and direct club costs.

Team setup

Members can request a singlet, join the Facebook group, connect on Strava, and use Discord to coordinate long runs, workouts, races, and results.

Coastal Run Maine

Dirigo members receive year-round Coastal Run Maine discounts: 25% off shoes, 10% off fuel, and 10% off apparel.

USATF

Dirigo is registered with USATF New England as team code 02-0225. Maine residents may need to transfer from USATF Maine to USATF New England before scoring for the team.

Common membership questions.

Do I need to meet a time standard?

No. Dirigo has no time or age requirements for membership. Specific races or team scoring opportunities may have their own requirements.

Can I come to a group run before joining?

Yes, when a run is listed as public. Check current details first, since some team activity may be informal, seasonal, or race-specific.

What does membership include?

Team membership, club communications, racing coordination, a team singlet, and connection to other Maine runners. Adult dues are $40 per year. Current benefits also include Coastal Run Maine discounts on shoes, fuel, and apparel.

Who do I contact?

Use the membership link to join or renew. For specific questions, message Dirigo on Instagram.
